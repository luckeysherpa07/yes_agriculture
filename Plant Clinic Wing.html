Plant Clinic Wing

Plant clinic is basically a one roof spot where farmers seeking solution to their various problems related with cultivation are answered by local plant health extension officers known as “Plant Doctors”.The major aim of such clinic is to diagnose pests and diseases in crops and provide accurate knowledge to the farming communities.Agricultural sector is the pivotal point for enhancing economic growth,ensuring food security and reducing poverty, but in country like Nepal agricultural services are beyond the access of common farmers.Considering this different problems of farmers, YES Agriculture took an inception to start up Plant Clinic to provide an array of technological solutions to farmers more systematically,precisely and swiftly   by integrating multiple sectors linked with agricultural services

:  Major Objectives of Plant Clinic Wing
  . To diagnose the plant samples brought by the farmers for various entomological,pathological,nutritional,physiological and agronomic problems  • 
 To provide technological solutions and remedial measures to the farmers proble  • 
.To suggest plant protection and plant production packages for different crop  • 
.To make field visit and extend the knowledge beyond books and provide practical training for undergraduate students during studies  •

: Programs Launched by Plant Clinic Wing
 As the initiation of Plant Clinic,the founder members of YES Agriculture under the presidentship of Mr.Dinesh Raj Pant and other founder members including Mr.ManojKadel,Mr. ArjunBatola,Mr. Om PrakashChaudhary, Mr. Ramesh Uprety and Miss PoonamSapkota conducted “PLANT CLINIC CAMPAIGN”           
near the vicinity of IAAS inMeudihawa,Rupandehi

 After the grand success of the above campaign a separate wing, Plant Clinic Wingwas established under the coordinatorship of Mr.GauravBhattarai.Under his coordinatorship this program was extended to other areas of Rupandehi in Sainamaina Municipality along with Meudihawa,Rupandehi

There after under the co-ordinatorship of Mr. Rabin Rijalthisprogram was amended realizing the interrelationship of soil & plants and soil test was also included along with plant clinic .“PLANT CLINIC AND SOIL TESTING CAMAPAIGN-2073” was conducted in Manpakadi-1,Babhanidanda,Rupandehi in  January,2017 Forty nine plant samples along with 108 soil samples were tested
