Veterinary Wings 

‘YES Agriculture’, the name of the organization suggests that it is an organization working only in the field of agriculture but this very concept was proven wrong through the establishment of veterinary wing in this organization. IAAS is an old institute but the addition of veterinary faculty in this Paklihawa is very recent. Not one full fledge batch of veterinary students have passed out through IAAS, Paklihawa. In such scenario of developing faculty this wing was established to provide and assist the practical knowledge for the veterinary students. As the college was developing its very own veterinary faculty, this wing of the organization provided the helping hand in the development from the vaccination campaign (Rabies, PPR) to awareness campaign. Students have been directly benefited from the activities of this wing. It has provided a learning platform as well as a place to apply the theoretical knowledge gained into practice. When the college was lagging behind to organize such beneficiary programs for the students, the veterinary wing of this organization stepped up to take an initiation to organize host of programs. Not only the students but the farmers in the locality are also benefited by the works of this wing. Providing much needed awareness and all so essential vaccines to the local farmers for the betterment of their livestock. ‘YES agriculture’ has certainly left a lasting mark on the people around
