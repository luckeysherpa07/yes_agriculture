Youth Innovation Wings 

Youth for Endeavour and Sustainable Agriculture (YES Agriculture) was established in 2069 B.S.( write full date here) by our founder and the very first president Mr. Dinesh Pant along with his friends. This organization was established with the motive of bringing positive changes in the field of agriculture by youth itself. Hence youths are the main pillar of this organization. A new concept of wings was emerged and six wings were established in (write date here) which are : Youth Innovation, Plant Clinic, Waste Management, ICT, Literature and Veterinary Wings.
Youth Innovation Wing was initiated to explore youths power and ability. This wing focuses most of its programmes for youth welfare and boost up their confidence and their knowledge. On 12th August i.e. on Youth International Day, our wing conducted an interaction programme with our honorable guests (Scientist, DADO and DLSO officers, Campus Chief, Professors, and so on). After new committee formation, I was bestowed with the opportunity to be the co-ordinator of Youth Innovation wing to work for the welfare of youth in co-ordination with my Vice Co-ordinator and three members. Our committee has been publishing set of questions related to agriculture and veterinary science fortnightly to give some ideas about Lok Sewa questions to IAASians and expand their horizon of knowledge since few months. We have also organized Basic Agroforestry Training with the help of Mr. Hari Gaire in Chiliya VDC with farmers. After our encouraging success in farmers field, we organized same program in our college IAAS and about 50 students participated. The success of our every program is a sign of co-ordination and cordial relation among us hence I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to all the innovative minds and helping hands for their support and believing on me. I wish the upcoming committee a successful tenure and I am sure our committee will reach a new height owing to the dedication of its new innovative faces committee. Thank You!!!
