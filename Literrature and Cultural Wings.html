Literrature and Cultural Wings 

Youth Endeavour for Sustainable Agriculture (YES Agriculture) has been organizing various programs in the field of Veterinary and Agriculture since its establishment. Realising the interest and utmost need of the students, Literature And Cultural Wing was established to provide a common platform for art and literature lovers to express their hidden talent.The primary objective of this wing is to emphasize the role of art and literature in agricultural awareness and extension programs.In the very first year of its establishment,Literature and Cultural Wing has organized various programs which we believe have assisted the agriculture and veterinary studying students in one or the other way. The first program of the wing was“Khulla Rachana Wachan tatha Bidaai Karyakram” (Poetry Recitation And Farewell Program) which was certainly a huge success as more than thirty students of the campus had recited their poems and ghazals in the program.On the occasion of “World Environment Day-2016”, it had organized “Khulla Baktitwakala Pratiyogita”(Free Speech Competition) entitled “Role of Agriculture and Veterinary In Wildlife Conservation”.Similarly, the wing had organised a drama in Meudihawa village to make local people aware of fatal disease Rabies during “Anti-Rabies Campaign Week 2073-Second Edition” program organized by Veterinary wing of this organization. In collaberation with “Lisno Sahitya Samaj” , a literary group of University Of College of Medical Sciences(UCMS) , the wing had conducted “Khulla Gajal ra Kabita Wachan Karyakram”(Poetry Recitation Program) on the occasion of Bhanu Jayanti in UCMS,Ranigaun.At present, the wing has been conducting a peculiar informal program “Chautareema Pratibha’’ where art and literature lovers of the campus gather together and recite poems,     ghazals and sing songs.This program is being conducted in every fifteen days each time in a different chautaree
 Literature is not only my field of interest but my passion. Working as coordinator of Literature And Cultural Wing has been a matter of pride to myself as it has helped me to be a better version of myself. I believe, our wing has played a vital role in bringing art and literature lovers together for promoting co-operation and understanding among them.
